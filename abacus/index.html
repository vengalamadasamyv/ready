FAQ

Learning Abacus provides numerous benefits, including improved mental calculation skills, enhanced memory, better concentration, and faster problem-solving abilities. It also boosts logical thinking and visual-spatial memory, which are essential for academic performance, especially in mathematics. Additionally, Abacus helps build confidence and discipline, making students more capable in various areas of learning and daily life.

The duration of an Abacus course depends on the level of the course and the student's learning pace. Generally, basic courses may take 3 to 6 months to complete, while intermediate and advanced courses may require 1 to 2 years. Regular practice and dedication can accelerate progress and mastery of skills.

Abacus courses are typically suitable for children aged 5 to 14 years. However, adults can also benefit from Abacus training as it helps enhance mental calculation, focus, and cognitive skills. Early learners can develop strong foundational math skills, while older students and adults can improve speed and accuracy in mental arithmetic.
