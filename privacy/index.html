Privacy and Policy


At Nethra Shree Kids & Culturals Academy ("NSKC Academy"), located in Kalugumalai, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you use our services, including online and offline daycare, skill development, and tuition programs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us when you register on our platform, enroll in our courses, or interact with us in other ways. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Course Enrollment Information: Details about the courses you enroll in, your progress, and completion status.
  • Payment Information: Billing details and payment history if you opt for paid services such as certificates or premium subscriptions.
  • Device and Usage Information: Information about the device and browser you use to access our platform, IP address, etc.

2. How We Use Your Information

NSKC Academy uses the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide you with access to our courses and services.
  • To process payments for any paid services or products.
  • To personalize your learning experience based on your preferences and progress.
  • To send you important notifications related to your account, including updates on your course progress.
  • To improve our website, services, and support to better serve our users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in running the platform and providing our services, such as payment processors or email service providers.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a legal process or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Security

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. NSKC Academy employs appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption and secure servers to store sensitive data.

5. Cookies

NSKC Academy uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience on our platform. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device and help us remember your preferences and improve the performance of our website.

By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but please note that disabling cookies may affect your user experience.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request to view the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email we send.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

NSKC Academy does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

NSKC Academy reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will take effect as of the date it is posted. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
